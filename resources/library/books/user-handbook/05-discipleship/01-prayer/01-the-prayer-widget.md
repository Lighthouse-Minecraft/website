When you log in to your Lighthouse dashboard, you'll see a **Spiritual Discipleship** section with the **Pray Today** widget. This is your daily prayer companion.

## How It Works

Each day, the widget shows a different country or region for you to pray for. The goal is simple -- take a moment to pray for that nation and the people who live there.

When you're ready, click the **I Prayed Today** button to record your prayer. That's it! The button will update to show that you've prayed, and your participation will be counted alongside other Lighthouse members.

## Your Prayer Streak

The widget tracks your **prayer streak** -- the number of consecutive days you've prayed. Your streak is shown with a lightning bolt icon next to your count.

If you miss a day, your streak resets to zero. But don't worry about it -- just pick back up the next day. The streak is there to encourage consistency, not to create pressure.

## Community Participation

Next to your streak, you'll see a count of how many Lighthouse members have prayed today. This is a great reminder that you're not praying alone -- the whole community is joining together.

## Prayer Resources

Below the button, you'll find two helpful links:

- **Prayer Details** -- Opens the Operation World page for today's country, where you can learn more about what to pray for
- **PrayerCast Video** -- A short video to guide your prayer for today's nation (not available for every country)

There's also a link to the **Lighthouse Prayer List**, where staff members can share prayer requests for the ministry.

## The Prayer Graph

Next to the prayer widget, you'll see a **community prayer graph**. This chart shows how many members have prayed each day over the past week. It's encouraging to watch the community grow in prayer together.
