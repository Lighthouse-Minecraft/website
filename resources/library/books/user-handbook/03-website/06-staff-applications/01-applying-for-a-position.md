## Overview

If you're interested in serving on the Lighthouse staff team, you can apply for open positions directly from the website. The process is straightforward -- find a position, read the info, answer some questions, and submit.

## Before You Apply

A few things to know before you start:

- You can only have **one active application** at a time. If you already have an application in progress, you'll need to wait for it to be resolved before applying for something else.
- If a previous application was denied, you're welcome to apply again later.
- You must be a member in good standing -- if your account has been restricted by staff, you won't be able to submit an application.

## Finding Open Positions

1. Visit the [Staff Page]({{url:/staff}})
2. Browse the available positions by department
3. Click on a position to see its details -- title, department, rank, description, responsibilities, and requirements
4. If the position is currently accepting applications, you'll see an **Apply for this Position** button

Not every position is open for applications at all times. If you don't see the Apply button, that position isn't currently accepting new applicants.

## Submitting Your Application

1. Click **Apply for this Position** on the staff page
2. You'll see an **information screen** explaining what to expect from the application process -- read through it and click **Continue**
3. Fill out the application form -- every question is required, so make sure you answer each one thoughtfully
4. When you're ready, click **Submit Application**

The questions you see depend on the type of position you're applying for. Some questions are the same for everyone, while others are specific to the role or department.

## What Happens Next

Once you submit:

- You'll receive an **email confirmation** that your application was received
- Staff will review your application and move it through the process
- You'll get email updates as your application status changes

The review process may include an interview stage. If that happens, you'll be notified and a discussion thread will be created where you can communicate with the review team.

## Tips for a Strong Application

- **Be honest and thoughtful** -- staff reviewers are looking for genuine answers, not perfect ones
- **Take your time** -- there's no rush to submit. Think through your responses before hitting Submit
- **Show your heart** -- Lighthouse is a community, and we want to know who you are and why you want to serve
- If you've applied before, your previous answers will be pre-filled to save you time. Feel free to update them.
