## Who Can Comment

To leave a comment on a blog post, you need to be logged in and have a [[books/user-handbook/website/your-account/membership-levels|membership level]] of **Traveler** or above. If you're not logged in, you'll see a prompt to log in at the bottom of each post.

If your account is currently in [[books/user-handbook/website/the-brig/what-is-the-brig|the Brig]], commenting is temporarily disabled until you're released.

## Leaving a Comment

1. Open the blog post you'd like to comment on.
2. Scroll down to the **Comments** section below the article.
3. Type your comment in the text box. You can use basic markdown formatting (bold, italics, links).
4. Click **Post Comment**.

## Comment Moderation

To keep conversations respectful and on-topic, some comments go through a brief moderation review before they appear publicly. If your comment is held for moderation, you'll see a message letting you know it's been submitted. A staff member or blog author will review it, and once approved, it will appear on the post.

Established, long-standing members may have their comments posted immediately without a moderation wait.

## Tips for Good Comments

- Stay on topic -- relate your comment to the blog post.
- Be respectful and constructive, even if you disagree with something.
- Keep it friendly -- remember that people of all ages are part of this community.
