## Finding the Blog

You can reach the blog from the **Blog** link in the sidebar navigation, or by visiting [the blog page]({{url:/blog}}) directly. No account is needed -- the blog is open to everyone.

## Browsing Posts

Posts appear in reverse chronological order, with the newest posts first. Each post card shows the title, author, publication date, category, a short excerpt, and any tags. Click a post title or the "Read more" link to open the full article.

## Filtering Posts

You can narrow down what you see in a few ways:

- **By category** -- Use the category sidebar on the right side of the blog page. Each category shows how many published posts it contains.
- **By tag** -- Click any tag badge on a post card to see all posts with that tag.
- **By author** -- Click an author's name on any post to see everything they've written.

When you're filtering, a "Back to all posts" link appears at the top so you can easily return to the full listing.

## Reading a Post

On a post page you'll see the full article, including any images and embedded community stories. Below the article there are buttons to share the post on Twitter/X, Facebook, or copy the link to your clipboard.

Scroll down past the article to find the comments section, where members discuss the post.

## RSS Feed

If you use an RSS reader, you can subscribe to the Lighthouse blog. Click the **RSS** button in the top-right corner of the blog page to get the feed URL. New posts will show up in your reader automatically.
