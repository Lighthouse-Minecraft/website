## Who Can Suggest

If you're a **Citizen**, you can suggest new questions for the community. This option appears at the bottom of the **Current Stories** tab in a card labeled **Suggest a Question**.

## How to Suggest a Question

1. Scroll to the **Suggest a Question** card at the bottom of the Current Stories tab
2. Type your question into the text field (minimum 10 characters)
3. Click **Suggest**

Your suggestion is sent to staff for review. If they approve it, a draft question is created for them to schedule. You won't see your suggestion appear immediately -- staff will decide when to use it.

## Tips for Good Suggestions

Think about questions that would spark interesting conversations and bring the community together. Open-ended questions that let people share personal experiences tend to work best. Avoid questions with simple yes/no answers or anything too narrow.
