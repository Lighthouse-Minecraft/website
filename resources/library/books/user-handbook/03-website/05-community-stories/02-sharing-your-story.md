## Responding to the Current Question

When there's an active community question, you'll see it displayed at the top of the **Current Stories** tab (and on your Dashboard widget). To share your story:

1. Read the question and any description that goes with it
2. Write your response in the text box under **Share Your Story** (minimum 20 characters)
3. Optionally attach an image by clicking the file upload field (max 2 MB)
4. Click **Submit Response**

Your story is sent to staff for review. Once approved, it will appear on the page for everyone to read. You'll see a message confirming your story has been submitted.

## One Response Per Question

You can only submit one response per question. Once you've shared your story, the form is replaced with a note that you've already responded. If you need to make changes, you can edit your response as long as staff hasn't reviewed it yet (see below).

## Editing or Deleting Your Response

If your response is still waiting for review, you can edit or delete it:

- Click **Edit** on your response to open the editor
- Update your text or change the attached image
- Click **Save** when you're done

Once staff has approved or rejected your response, you can no longer edit it.

## Attaching Images

You can include one image with your response. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, GIF, and other standard image types. The maximum file size is 2 MB. If you need to remove an image after submitting, use the edit option.
