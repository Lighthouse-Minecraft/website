## The Community Logbook

The **Community Logbook** is a place where Lighthouse members can share stories, memories, and experiences with each other. Staff regularly post community questions -- things like "What's your favorite Lighthouse memory?" or "What does community mean to you?" -- and you write your response.

After staff reviews and approves your story, it appears on the page for everyone to read and react to. Over time, the Logbook becomes a growing archive of shared experiences from across the community.

## Where to Find It

You'll see the current question on your **Dashboard** in the **Community Question** widget. You can also visit the full [Community Stories]({{url:/community-stories}}) page to read stories, browse past questions, and more.

There's no link in the main navigation -- the Dashboard widget is the main entry point.

## Who Can Participate

Your [[books/user-handbook/website/your-account/membership-levels|membership level]] determines what you can do:

| Level | What You Can Do |
|---|---|
| **Traveler** and above | View stories, respond to the current question, react to stories |
| **Resident** and above | Everything above, plus respond to one past question per cycle |
| **Citizen** and above | Everything above, plus suggest new questions for staff to consider |

If your account is currently in [[books/user-handbook/website/the-brig/what-is-the-brig|the Brig]], you won't be able to access Community Stories until you're released.
