## Reading Stories

Once staff approves community responses, they appear in the story feed on the **Current Stories** tab. Each story shows the author's name, avatar, their response text, any attached image, and when it was approved.

You can scroll through all the approved stories for the current question. If there are many stories, the list is paginated -- use the page controls at the bottom to browse more.

## Emoji Reactions

You can react to any approved story using one of six emoji reactions:

- Click any emoji button below a story to add your reaction
- Click the same emoji again to remove your reaction
- You can add multiple different reactions to the same story

Reactions are visible to everyone and show a count of how many people have reacted with each emoji.

## Past Questions

The **Past Questions** tab shows all the questions that have been asked and archived. Click **View Stories** on any past question to expand it and read the approved responses.

## Responding to Past Questions

If you're a **Resident** or above, you can also respond to one past question per cycle. Here's how it works:

- You must have answered the current active question first
- You can respond to one archived question per cycle (a cycle resets each time a new question becomes active)
- Click **View Stories** on a past question, then use the **Respond to This Question** form at the bottom

If you don't see the response form, it's either because you haven't answered the current question yet or you've already responded to a past question this cycle.
