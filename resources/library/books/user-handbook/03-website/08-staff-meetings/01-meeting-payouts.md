## What Are Meeting Payouts?

When a staff meeting is completed, the system automatically sends **Lumens** -- our in-game currency -- to eligible staff members as a thank-you for their participation. You don't need to do anything special to claim them; if you qualify, they appear in your Minecraft account shortly after the meeting wraps up.

## Who Gets Paid?

Eligibility depends on your staff rank:

| Rank | Requirements to Receive Lumens |
|------|-------------------------------|
| Junior Crew Member | Submit your **staff update form** before the meeting |
| Crew Member | Submit your staff update form before the meeting |
| Officer | Submit your staff update form AND attend the meeting |

If you don't have a Minecraft account linked to your Lighthouse profile, your payout will be skipped -- the system can't send Lumens without knowing your in-game name. Make sure your account is linked in your profile settings before the meeting.

## What Counts as Submitting the Form?

The **staff update form** is the report you fill out before each staff meeting. It's available on the meeting page once the submission window opens. Submitting it means clicking the submit button on the form -- just saving a draft doesn't count.

If you're not sure where to find the form, check the meeting page linked in the staff announcements before each meeting.

## What Could Cause Me to Be Skipped?

There are a few reasons your payout might be skipped:

- **Form not submitted** -- You didn't submit your staff update form in time
- **Did not attend** -- Officers who weren't marked as attending the meeting
- **No Minecraft account** -- You don't have a linked and active Minecraft account
- **Rank payout disabled** -- Admins can set certain ranks' payout to zero; if so, no one of that rank receives Lumens that meeting
- **Excluded by manager** -- The meeting manager excluded you manually (this is uncommon and would usually be discussed with you)

## When Do the Lumens Arrive?

Lumens are sent when the meeting manager clicks "Complete Meeting." You'll receive them on your **primary Minecraft account** -- the one set as your main account on your profile. You may need to be online at the time, or they may appear the next time you join, depending on how the economy plugin handles offline players.

## Viewing the Payout Record

After a meeting is completed, you can see who was paid, who was skipped, and why by visiting the meeting page. Look for the **Payout Summary** section at the bottom of the completed meeting page. It shows the full results of that meeting's payouts.

## Troubleshooting

### I qualified but didn't receive my Lumens
First, check the Payout Summary on the meeting page to see your entry and status. If your status shows "Failed," the RCON connection had a problem sending your payment -- contact a meeting manager to have it processed manually. If it shows "Skipped," check the reason listed.

### I forgot to submit my form
Unfortunately, there's no back-pay for missed forms. Make sure to submit before the next meeting to qualify. The submission window opens a week before the meeting date.
