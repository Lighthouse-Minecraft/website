## What It Is

Lighthouse publishes monthly financial summaries so anyone -- members, parents, or curious visitors -- can see how ministry funds are received and spent. This is part of our commitment to running an open and accountable community.

You don't need an account to view this page. Visit it anytime at [Finances]({{url:/finances}}).

## What You'll See

At the top of the page, you'll find a **year-to-date summary** -- a running total of income, expenses, and net change for the current year across all published months.

Below that, each completed month is listed separately, showing:

- **Total Income** -- all funds received during that month
- **Total Expenses** -- all funds spent during that month
- **Net** -- the difference (income minus expenses)

All amounts are from officially **published** months. Months where the treasurer hasn't finished reviewing and publishing the report don't appear here -- you'll only ever see finalized data.

## More Detail for Members

Residents and higher can see a **category breakdown** for each month. This shows which areas of the ministry the income came from and where the expenses went -- things like infrastructure, software, or administration. This gives you a clearer picture of how funds flow through the ministry.

Staff with full financial access can see even more detail, including individual transaction categories and counts.

## Why We Do This

Running a ministry transparently isn't just good practice -- it helps the community trust that funds are handled responsibly. Lighthouse is committed to clear reporting so that members, families, and donors can feel confident about how the ministry operates.

If you have questions about a specific period or want to know more, you're welcome to reach out to staff via a [[books/user-handbook/website/getting-help/support-tickets|support ticket]].
