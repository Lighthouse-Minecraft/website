## Your Profile Page

Every member has a public **profile page** that shows your display name, avatar, membership level, and any linked Minecraft or Discord accounts. Other members can visit your profile to see this information.

## Settings

You can manage your account by going to **Settings** (click your avatar in the top-right corner and choose Settings). Here's what you'll find:

### Profile

This is where you manage your basic account information:

- **Name** -- Your public display name
- **Email** -- The email address used for login and notifications. If you change your email, you'll need to verify the new one
- **Timezone** -- Set your timezone so that dates and times across the site are shown in your local time
- **Avatar Source** -- Choose where your profile picture comes from:
  - **Auto** -- Uses your Minecraft skin first, then Discord avatar, then your initials
  - **Minecraft Account** -- Always uses your primary Minecraft account's skin
  - **Discord Account** -- Always uses your linked Discord avatar
  - **Gravatar** -- Uses the Gravatar service based on your email

### Password

Change your password here. You'll need to enter your current password first, then your new password twice to confirm.

### Appearance

Choose between **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** theme. The System option follows whatever your device is set to.

### Deleting Your Account

If you ever need to delete your account, you can do so from the Profile settings page. This is permanent and requires your password to confirm. Once deleted, your account and all associated data are removed.
