## What You'll Need

To create a Lighthouse account, you'll need:

- A **username** (this is your public display name on the site)
- An **email address** (used for login and notifications)
- Your **date of birth** (used to ensure age-appropriate safety settings)
- A **password**

That's it -- the process takes just a couple of minutes.

## Signing Up

Head to the registration page and fill out your details. Your username is how other community members will see you, so pick something you're comfortable with.

We ask for your date of birth so we can apply the right safety settings for your age. This information helps us keep the community safe for everyone.

## For Users Under 17

If you're under 17, you'll see an extra step asking for a **parent or guardian email address**. We'll send your parent information about your account and how to manage it.

Your parent doesn't need to do anything for you to get started -- you can begin exploring right away. However, your parent will have the ability to manage certain permissions on your account, like whether you can link Minecraft or Discord accounts.

## What Happens Next

Once you've registered, you'll land on your **Dashboard** as a **Drifter** -- that's the starting membership level for all new accounts.

The first thing you'll see is a prompt to read and accept the community rules. Once you've read them and clicked the accept button, you'll be promoted to **Stowaway** automatically. From there, you can start linking your Discord account and exploring the website.

After you are a **Stowaway** (have agreed to the rules) staff members will be notified to review your account. If everything checks out they will promote you to **Traveler**, granting you full access to the community, including our Discord and Minecraft servers.

For more details on how membership levels work and what each one unlocks, check out the [[books/user-handbook/website/your-account/membership-levels|Membership Levels]] page.
