## The Discord Step

When you're a **Stowaway**, the first thing the wizard asks you to do is connect your Discord account. Linking Discord lets Lighthouse assign you the right roles on the server automatically and enables Discord DM notifications for things like ticket replies and announcements.

Click **Connect Discord** and you'll be taken to Discord's authorization page. Once you authorize the connection, you'll be brought straight back to your Dashboard -- the wizard will detect your linked account and move you to the next step automatically. There's nothing else you need to do.

For a detailed walkthrough of the linking process itself, see [[books/user-handbook/discord/accounts/linking-your-account|Linking Your Discord Account]].

## If Discord Is Disabled

If a parent or guardian has turned off Discord access for your account, the wizard will show you a message explaining that. Just click **Continue** to move past this step -- your Lighthouse account setup can still be completed without Discord.

## Skipping Discord

You don't have to link Discord right now. Click **Skip for now** and the wizard will move on to the waiting step. You can always link your Discord account later from your Settings.

## After This Step

Once you've connected Discord (or skipped it), the wizard shows the waiting card while staff reviews your account. See [[books/user-handbook/getting-started/account-setup/waiting-for-approval|Waiting for Approval]] to know what to expect next.
