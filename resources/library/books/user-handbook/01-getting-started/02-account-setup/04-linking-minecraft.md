## The Minecraft Step

Once you're a **Traveler**, the wizard's final step asks you to link your Minecraft account. This is what gets you onto the Lighthouse server -- linking your account adds you to the whitelist automatically, so you can log in and start playing.

Click **Connect Minecraft** and you'll be taken to your account settings to complete the process. For a full walkthrough, see [[books/user-handbook/minecraft/accounts/linking-your-account|Linking Your Minecraft Account]].

## If Minecraft Is Disabled

If a parent or guardian has turned off Minecraft access for your account, the wizard will show a message explaining that. Click **Continue** to finish your account setup. If your parent later enables Minecraft access, you can link your account from Settings.

## Skipping Minecraft

You can skip this step and do it later. Click **Skip for now** and the wizard will mark your setup as complete. You can link your Minecraft account anytime from your **Settings** under **Minecraft Accounts**.

## Completing Your Setup

Once you link your Minecraft account (or skip the step), the wizard is finished. A **"Welcome to Lighthouse!"** screen will appear with some suggestions for what to do next:

- **Join Discussions** in the community
- **Open a Ticket** if you need help
- **Explore Community Content** like announcements and blog posts

The welcome screen also includes a link to your **notification preferences** so you can set up how you'd like to hear from us. Click **Get Started** to close it and see your full Dashboard.

## What's Unlocked

As a Traveler with a linked Minecraft account, you now have full access to the community:

- You're whitelisted on the Lighthouse Minecraft server
- Your in-game rank is synced with your membership level
- You have full access to all Discord channels
- You can participate in all community features on the website

Welcome to Lighthouse!
