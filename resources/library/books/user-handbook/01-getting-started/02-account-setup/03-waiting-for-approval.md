## What This Step Is

After you've handled the Discord step (either connected your account, skipped it, or continued past a disabled account), the wizard shows a **waiting card**. This is where you'll be as a **Stowaway** while staff reviews your account and promotes you to **Traveler**.

There's nothing you need to do here -- just wait. Staff typically review new accounts within a day or two.

## What Staff Is Looking For

Staff will take a look at your account to make sure everything looks good before approving you as a Traveler. This is a normal part of how Lighthouse keeps the community safe and welcoming. You don't need to do anything special -- just be yourself.

## While You Wait

Feel free to explore the rest of the Lighthouse website. You can browse announcements, read the community handbook, or check out the [[books/user-handbook/website/getting-help/support-tickets|support tickets]] section if you have any questions for staff.

## When You're Approved

When a staff member promotes you to Traveler, the waiting card disappears the next time you visit your Dashboard. You'll see the **Minecraft step** instead, which guides you through linking your Minecraft account so you can join the server.

You'll also receive an email letting you know you've been promoted.

## Troubleshooting

### It's been a while and I'm still waiting

If several days have passed and you haven't heard anything, try opening a [[books/user-handbook/website/getting-help/support-tickets|support ticket]]. Staff can check on the status and let you know if anything is needed from you.
