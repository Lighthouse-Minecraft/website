## What It Is

When you're a brand-new member, your **Dashboard** shows a setup wizard instead of the normal community content. It's a short, step-by-step guide that walks you through connecting your accounts so you're ready to participate in the community.

The wizard appears for **Stowaway** and **Traveler** members and disappears automatically once you've finished -- or if you choose to dismiss it.

## Before the Wizard: Accepting the Community Rules

When you first create an account, you'll see a **Read & Accept Rules** button on your Dashboard. You need to read and accept the Lighthouse Community Rules before the setup wizard begins.

Click the button to open the rules. Once you've read them, click **I Have Read the Rules and Agree to Follow Them** at the bottom. Your account will move to **Stowaway** status right away and the wizard will appear on your next visit.

If you're a younger member, your parent can also accept the rules on your behalf from the Parent Portal.

## The Steps

The wizard takes you through up to three steps in order:

1. **Connect Discord** -- Link your Discord account so Lighthouse can assign you the right server roles and send you notifications. This step is shown when you're a Stowaway.
2. **Wait for approval** -- After the Discord step, you'll see a waiting card while staff reviews your account and promotes you to Traveler. Nothing to do here -- just hang tight.
3. **Link Minecraft** -- Once you're a Traveler, you'll be guided through linking your Minecraft account to get whitelisted on the server. This is the final step.

You don't have to complete every step in one sitting. The wizard saves your progress and picks up where you left off.

## Your Options on Each Step

On each step, you have a few choices:

- **Complete the step** -- Click the main button to go do that thing (like connecting your Discord account). When you come back to the Dashboard the wizard will have moved on automatically.
- **Skip for now** -- If you'd rather do it later, you can skip the current step and move on. You can always do it later from your Settings.
- **Dismiss** -- If you don't want to see the wizard at all, the Dismiss button in the top corner will remove it permanently. You can still do everything it suggests from your [[books/user-handbook/website/your-account/your-profile-and-settings|Settings]] page at any time.

## The Welcome Screen

When you finish the Minecraft step (or skip it), a **"Welcome to Lighthouse!"** screen pops up. It highlights some things you can do now that your account is set up -- like joining community discussions, opening a support ticket if you need help, or exploring community content. It also includes a link to your notification preferences so you can choose how you'd like to hear from us.

Click **Get Started** to close it and see your full Dashboard.

## Don't Want the Wizard?

That's totally fine. Click **Dismiss** on any step and the wizard goes away. Everything the wizard helps you set up -- Discord linking, Minecraft linking -- is still available from your Settings anytime.

If the wizard is still active, you'll also see a **Resume Account Setup** link in the sidebar so you can always find your way back to it.
