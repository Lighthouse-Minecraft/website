# Welcome to Lighthouse

Lighthouse is a **Christian Minecraft ministry and community**. We provide a safe, welcoming environment for players of all ages to enjoy Minecraft together while growing in faith.

## What We Offer

- A friendly and moderated Minecraft server
- Community events and activities
- Mentorship and discipleship opportunities
- A supportive online community

## Getting Help

If you need assistance at any time, you can:

1. Open a **[support ticket](https://www.lighthousemc.net/tickets)** through the website
2. Ask a staff member in-game or on Discord
3. Browse this handbook for answers to common questions
