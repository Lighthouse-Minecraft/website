# Creating Your First Land Claim

Once you find a place you want to build, the first step is to create your own **Land**.

This only takes a moment.

## Step 1: Stand Where You Want Your Land

Go to the area where you want your base or build to be.  
Your land claim will be created **at your current location**.

## Step 2: Create Your Land

Use the following command: `/lands create <name>`

Example: `/lands create myIsland`

Choose a name you’ll recognize. Many players name their land after their base or settlement.

## Step 3: Claim the Chunk

After creating your land, you can start claiming the chunks around you.

Open the land menu with: `/lands menu`


From there you can claim chunks and begin protecting the area around your base.

## What Is a Chunk?

Minecraft divides the world into square areas called **chunks**.

Each chunk is **16 × 16 blocks** wide.  
When you claim land, you are claiming entire chunks.

This keeps the system simple and helps prevent confusing boundaries.

## Tips

- Start by claiming the chunk where your **main base** is located.
- Claim extra chunks if you plan to expand.
- Leave space between other players’ land unless you’ve agreed to build together.

In the next section, we’ll look at **how to expand your land as your base grows**.
