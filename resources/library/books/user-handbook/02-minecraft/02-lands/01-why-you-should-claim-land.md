# Why You Should Claim Land

When you build something in Lighthouse, you probably want it to stay **safe and untouched**. That’s where the **Lands** system comes in.

Claiming land lets you mark an area of the world as **yours**. Once land is claimed, other players can’t break blocks, open chests, or interact with things there unless you give them permission.

This protects your:
- Houses and bases
- Farms and animals
- Storage and valuables
- Redstone builds and machines

It also helps prevent **accidental damage**. Even friendly players exploring nearby won’t be able to break things by mistake.

Claiming land is a normal part of playing on Lighthouse. Most players claim the area around their base so they can build freely without worrying about their work being changed by others.

You can also **share access** with friends, so they can help build and work on projects with you.
