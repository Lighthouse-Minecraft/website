## What is the Lighthouse Server?

The Lighthouse Minecraft server is a **whitelisted** community server. That means only approved players can join -- you won't run into random strangers. This keeps things safe and friendly for everyone, including younger players.

We support both **Java Edition** and **Bedrock Edition**, so you can play no matter which version of Minecraft you own.

## Before You Can Play

To join the server, you'll need two things:

1. **A Lighthouse website account** at the **Traveler** level or above, which means you have agreed to the rules and our staff have approved your account
2. **A linked Minecraft account** that has been verified

## How It Works

The process to get on the server has three steps:

1. **Link your Minecraft account** on the Lighthouse website
2. **Verify ownership** by joining the server and entering a code
3. **You're in!** Once verified, you're permanently whitelisted and can join anytime

The whole thing usually takes just a few minutes. The next page walks you through the linking process step by step.

## Java and Bedrock

You don't need to do anything special based on which edition you play. During the linking process, you'll pick whether your account is **Java** or **Bedrock**, and the system handles the rest. You can even link one of each if you play on both.
