## Your Rank Comes from the Website

Your in-game rank on the Lighthouse Minecraft server is tied directly to your **membership level** on the website. You don't need to do anything special in-game to earn a rank -- it happens automatically when staff promotes you on the website.

When your membership level changes, your in-game rank updates right away. No commands needed, no waiting around.

## The Ranks

Here's how website membership levels map to in-game ranks:

| Website Level | In-Game Rank | Server Access |
|---|---|---|
| **Drifter** | None | No access |
| **Stowaway** | None | No access |
| **Traveler** | Traveler | Full access |
| **Resident** | Resident | Full access |
| **Citizen** | Citizen | Full access |

**Drifters** and **Stowaways** can link their Minecraft accounts and go through the verification process, but they won't be able to join the server until a staff member reviews their profile and promotes them to **Traveler**. This is how we make sure new members are a good fit for the community before granting full access.

## What Each Rank Gets

### Traveler

This is your first in-game rank. As a **Traveler**, you have access to the server and can participate in everything the community has to offer. You can build, explore, and interact with other players.

### Resident

**Residents** are established members of the community. This rank is given to members who have been active and have shown they're a great part of the Lighthouse family. Residents have the same core access as Travelers, with the potential for additional features as the server evolves.

### Citizen

**Citizen** is the highest membership level. It's reserved for long-standing, trusted members of the community. Citizens may have access to special perks or privileges on the server.

## Staff Ranks

If you're a staff member on the Lighthouse website, your staff position is also reflected in-game. This helps other players know who to turn to if they need help while playing.

## How Promotions Work

You don't need to apply for a promotion -- staff members regularly review the community and promote members when they're ready. The best way to move up is to be an active, positive member of the community. Promotions happen naturally over time, so just enjoy playing and being part of the group.

If you've been around for a while and are curious about your membership level, you can check it on your profile page on the website.
