## What Are Discord Notifications?

If you have a linked Discord account, you can choose to receive certain Lighthouse notifications as **Discord direct messages**. This is a convenient way to stay in the loop without needing to check your email.

## Setting It Up

1. Make sure you have a [[books/user-handbook/discord/accounts/linking-your-account|linked Discord account]]
2. Go to **Settings** and click **Notifications**
3. For each notification type, toggle the **Discord DM** option on or off

If you don't have a linked Discord account, the Discord DM toggle will be disabled.

## What Notifications Can You Receive?

You can choose to receive Discord DMs for:

| Type | What You'll Get |
|---|---|
| **Ticket Updates** | Replies and status changes on your support tickets |
| **Account Updates** | Promotions and other changes to your account |
| **Announcements** | New community announcements |

Each type can be toggled independently, so you can pick exactly which ones you want as Discord messages.

## How It Works

When something happens that triggers a notification, the system checks your preferences. If you have Discord DMs enabled for that type, it sends you a direct message through the Lighthouse bot. The message includes a brief description of what happened and a link back to the website for more details.

Discord notifications work alongside your other notification channels -- you can have email and Discord DMs enabled at the same time, or use just one. For more details on all your notification options, see [[books/user-handbook/website/your-account/notification-preferences|Notification Preferences]].

## Troubleshooting

### I'm not receiving Discord DMs
Make sure your Discord privacy settings allow direct messages from server members. In Discord, go to **Privacy Settings** and enable **Allow direct messages from server members** for the Lighthouse server.

### The Discord toggle is grayed out
You need to link a Discord account first. Head to **Settings** > **Discord Account** to connect your account.
