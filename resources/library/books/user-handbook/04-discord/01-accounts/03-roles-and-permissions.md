## How Roles Work

When you link your Discord account to the Lighthouse website, the system automatically assigns you Discord roles that match your website [[books/user-handbook/website/your-account/membership-levels|membership level]]. These roles control which channels you can see and interact with on the Discord server.

You don't need to request roles or do anything manually -- they sync automatically whenever your membership level changes.

## Membership Roles

Your Discord role matches your current website membership level:

| Website Level | Discord Role | What It Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| **Stowaway** | -- | Access to the #foyer to introduce yourself |
| **Traveler** | Traveler | Full access to community channels |
| **Resident** | Resident | Additional channels and recognition |
| **Citizen** | Citizen | Highest community access |

You'll only have one membership role at a time. When you're promoted on the website, your old role is removed and the new one is added automatically.

## Verified Role

All linked accounts also receive a **Verified** role. This simply shows that your Discord account is connected to a real Lighthouse website account.

## Staff Roles

If you're a staff member, you'll also receive Discord roles for your **department** and **rank**. These are managed the same way -- assigned automatically when your staff position is set on the website, and updated if your position changes.

## When Do Roles Update?

Your roles are synced automatically when:

- You first link your Discord account
- You join the Discord server
- You're promoted or demoted on the website
- A staff member manually syncs your roles

In most cases, role changes happen within seconds. If you notice your roles seem out of date, head to **Settings** > **Discord Account** -- the page checks and syncs your roles when you visit it.

## What If My Roles Disappear?

There are a few reasons your Discord roles might be removed:

- **You were placed in the Brig** -- Your roles are temporarily removed while your account is restricted. They'll be restored when you're released.
- **A parent disabled Discord access** -- If a parent or guardian turns off Discord in the Parent Portal, your roles are removed until they re-enable it.
- **You unlinked your account** -- Unlinking removes all Lighthouse-managed roles from your Discord profile.

In all of these cases, your roles come back automatically once the situation is resolved. If something seems wrong, open a [[books/user-handbook/website/getting-help/support-tickets|support ticket]] and staff will help sort it out.
