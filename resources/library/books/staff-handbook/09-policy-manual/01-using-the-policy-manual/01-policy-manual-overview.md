## What the Policy Manual Is

The Lighthouse Policy Manual is the official collection of community policies. It is publicly accessible — no login required — which makes it a reliable reference you can share with anyone: current members, parents, prospective members, or people who found us through a search.

Every page in the manual carries a **Last updated** date so readers can see when the content was last revised.

## How It Is Organized

The manual has four parts, each with two chapters:

| Part | Chapter 1 | Chapter 2 |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| **Community Standards** | Code of Conduct | Community Expectations |
| **Safety & Privacy** | Child Safety Policy | Data Privacy Policy |
| **Moderation** | Moderation Practices | Discipline System |
| **Staff & Operations** | Staff Requirements | Operational Policies |

### Community Standards

**Code of Conduct** — The baseline rules for behavior in all Lighthouse spaces. Covers respectful communication, prohibited conduct, account ownership, and what happens when rules are broken.

**Community Expectations** — The positive values and habits Lighthouse expects of all members beyond just following rules: participation, supporting others, and being a constructive community presence.

### Safety & Privacy

**Child Safety Policy** — Our policies specifically designed to protect minors in the community, including supervision expectations, content standards, and reporting obligations.

**Data Privacy Policy** — How Lighthouse collects, uses, and protects member data. Covers what is stored, why, and members' rights regarding their own information.

### Moderation

**Moderation Practices** — How Lighthouse handles reports, makes moderation decisions, and communicates outcomes. Covers the guiding principles (fairness, consistency, good faith) and how to appeal a decision.

**Discipline System** — The Brig system and how formal discipline works, including what behaviors trigger Brig placement, what restrictions Brig members face, and how releases and appeals are handled.

### Staff & Operations

**Staff Requirements** — The qualifications, conduct standards, and confidentiality expectations for staff members. Also covers how staff join, step down, or are removed, and how members can report staff misconduct.

**Operational Policies** — How Lighthouse is run as a community: leadership structure, how policies are created and updated, financial policies around donations, and how significant changes are communicated.

## Who Can Edit the Policy Manual

Policy content is maintained by Command. Any staff member may raise a concern or suggest a clarification by submitting a Support Ticket or raising it in staff channels, but policy changes are drafted and approved by Command before publication.

The Policy Manual is stored as markdown files in the codebase. Updates require a code deploy — there is no web editor for production policy content. See [[books/staff-handbook/policy-manual/using-the-policy-manual/updating-policy-content|Updating Policy Content]] for the technical process.
