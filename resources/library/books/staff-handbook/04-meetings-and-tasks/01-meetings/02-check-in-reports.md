## Overview

Before each staff meeting, you'll be asked to fill out a **check-in report** answering questions set by the meeting organizer. These reports help the team prepare for the meeting and let leadership know what everyone's been working on.

## When Reports Are Available

Check-in reports unlock a configurable number of days before the meeting (typically 7 days). You can submit your report anytime between when it unlocks and when the meeting starts. Once the meeting begins, reports are locked and can no longer be edited.

## How to Submit Your Report

1. Go to the **Staff Ready Room**
2. Find the upcoming meeting on your **My Board** tab or in the **Upcoming Meetings** panel
3. Click the meeting to open it
4. You'll see the check-in questions -- answer each one
5. Click **Submit Report**

Your submission is timestamped. The Ready Room shows a checkmark next to meetings where you've already submitted.

## What Happens with Reports

- Meeting organizers and Officers can view all submitted reports before and during the meeting
- Reports help shape meeting discussions and identify items that need attention
- Automated reminders are sent to staff who haven't submitted their reports as the meeting date approaches (via email, Pushover, and Discord DM)

## Important Notes

- If you won't be at the meeting, submit your report anyway -- it's your way of keeping the team informed
- You can edit your report anytime before the meeting starts
- Once the meeting is in progress, your report is locked
