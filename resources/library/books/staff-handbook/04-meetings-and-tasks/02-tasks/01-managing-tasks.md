## Overview

Tasks are action items tracked by department. They live in the **Staff Ready Room** and can also be managed during meetings. Tasks help your team keep track of what needs to be done and who's doing it.

## Who Can Do This

- **Crew Members** and above can create, edit, complete, and archive tasks
- **Jr Crew Members** can view tasks but cannot create or modify them

## Where Tasks Live

Tasks appear in two places:

- **Staff Ready Room** -- the "My Board" tab shows tasks assigned to you, and each department tab shows that department's tasks
- **Meeting edit page** -- during meetings, department tasks are shown alongside meeting notes

## Creating a Task

1. Go to the **Staff Ready Room** and open your department's tab
2. Type the task description in the **Add Task** field at the bottom of the task list
3. Click **Add Task**

The task is created with a "Pending" status and assigned to your department. You can also create tasks during meetings -- they'll be linked to that meeting for tracking purposes.

## Assigning Tasks

Tasks can be assigned to a specific staff member:

1. Click the **edit** button (pencil icon) on a task
2. In the edit modal, select a staff member from the **Assign To** dropdown
3. Click **Save**

Assigned tasks show up on that person's "My Board" in the Ready Room. Unassigned tasks still appear in the department view for anyone to pick up.

## Completing Tasks

Click the **checkbox** next to a task to mark it complete. The system records who completed it and when. Completed tasks move to the "Recently Completed" section of the task list.

To undo a completion, click the checkbox again -- it toggles back to pending.

## Archiving Tasks

Archiving clears completed tasks from the active list while keeping a record. This option is only available **during meetings** -- when you're viewing tasks in a meeting context, completed tasks show an **Archive** button.

The archive keeps a record of which meeting a task was archived in, helping the Command department track productivity between meetings.

## Task Lifecycle

```text
Pending → Completed → Archived
```

- **Pending** -- needs to be done
- **Completed** -- done, but still visible in the "Recently Completed" section
- **Archived** -- cleared from the active list, permanently recorded

## Important Notes

- Tasks are organized by department (`section_key`) -- each department manages its own tasks independently
- The Command Dashboard uses task completion data across meeting iterations to track team engagement
- Tasks cannot be deleted -- they can only be archived once completed
