## Overview

The **Board Reports** page (`/finances/board-reports`) provides three standard financial statements for board review: Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement. Each can be viewed on-site and downloaded as a PDF.

## Who Can Do This

**Financials - Manage** role only.

## Setting the Date Range

At the top of the page, set a **Start Month** and **End Month**. All three reports update automatically when you change the range.

For quick access to standard reporting periods, use the trimester buttons:

- **T1 (Jan--Apr)** -- first trimester of the current year
- **T2 (May--Aug)** -- second trimester
- **T3 (Sep--Dec)** -- third trimester

## Income Statement

Shows income and expense by top-level category with subcategory detail, and a net income figure for the period. Click **Download PDF** to save it for board distribution.

## Balance Sheet

Shows each account's balance as of the last day of the end month, plus a net assets total. Balances include all transactions up to (and including) that date -- transfers between accounts are properly reflected. Click **Download PDF** to download.

## Cash Flow Statement

Shows operating activities (income and expense by category, with a net from operations) and financing activities (each inter-account transfer listed individually). Net cash change equals the operating net -- transfers between accounts net to zero for the ministry overall. Click **Download PDF** to download.

## Important Notes

- Board reports pull from all recorded transactions in the date range -- they are not limited to published months.
- PDF filenames include the date range for easy filing.
- These reports are intended for board and leadership review. Keep PDF copies in whatever archive the ministry uses for financial records.
