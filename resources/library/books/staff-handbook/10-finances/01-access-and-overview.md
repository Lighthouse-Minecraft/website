## Overview

The finance system has three access levels, each granting progressively more capability. A staff member needs to be assigned one of the finance roles before they can access the finance dashboard at all.

## Finance Roles

| Role | What You Can Do |
|---|---|
| **Financials - View** | Read the full transaction ledger, view account balances and category budgets, view and download period reports |
| **Financials - Treasurer** | Everything in View, plus record/edit/delete transactions, set monthly budgets, and publish period reports |
| **Financials - Manage** | Everything in Treasurer, plus create/rename/archive accounts, categories, and tags; generate board reports |

Each role is additive -- Treasurer has everything View has, and Manage has everything Treasurer has.

## Where to Find It

If you have a finance role, you'll find the **Finance** section linked from the Staff Ready Room. The main pages are:

- **Dashboard** (`/finances/dashboard`) -- transaction entry, ledger, account balances
- **Accounts** (`/finances/accounts`) -- account list and management
- **Categories** (`/finances/categories`) -- category and tag management
- **Budget** (`/finances/budget`) -- monthly planned vs. actual view
- **Reports** (`/finances/reports`) -- period report list and publishing
- **Board Reports** (`/finances/board-reports`) -- multi-period income statement, balance sheet, cash flow (Manage only)

## Finance Red Flag

If no period report has been published in the last 14 days, a **Finance Report Overdue** banner appears at the top of every staff meeting page. This is a reminder to the treasurer to catch up before the meeting. The banner includes a direct link to the finance dashboard and is visible to all staff who can view the meeting page.
