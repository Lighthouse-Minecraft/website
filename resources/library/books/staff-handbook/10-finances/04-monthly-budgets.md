## Overview

The **Budget** page (`/finances/budget`) lets the treasurer set planned amounts for each income and expense category. It shows planned vs. actual vs. a 3-month rolling trend so you can spot budget variances at a glance.

## Who Can Do This

- **Financials - View**: can view budgets (read-only)
- **Financials - Treasurer** and **Financials - Manage**: can edit and save planned amounts

## Navigating Months

Use the **← Previous** and **Next →** buttons to move between months. The page opens on the current month by default.

## Reading the Budget Table

Each top-level category has a row showing:

- **Category** -- the category name
- **Planned** -- the amount budgeted for this month (editable for Treasurer+)
- **Actual** -- what was actually recorded in transactions this month
- **Variance** -- planned minus actual (positive = under budget, negative = over budget)
- **3-Mo Trend** -- average actual spending over the three most recently published months

## Setting Planned Amounts

If you have the Treasurer role:

1. Go to `/finances/budget` for the month you want to set.
2. Click into any **Planned** field and type the planned amount in dollars.
3. Click **Save Budget** when done.

## Pre-Fill from Previous Month

When you open a month that has no budget data yet, the system automatically pre-fills the planned amounts from the previous month. This saves you from entering them from scratch each month -- just adjust what's different.

## Important Notes

- You can set planned amounts for future months in advance. This is useful for known upcoming expenses.
- The 3-month trend only uses **published** months -- unpublished months aren't included in the average.
- If fewer than three published months exist, the trend uses whatever is available.
