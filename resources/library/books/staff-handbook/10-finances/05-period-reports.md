## Overview

The **Reports** page (`/finances/reports`) shows a list of all months that have transactions, with their income and expense totals. When a month is ready to share with staff and the community, the treasurer publishes it -- which also locks all that month's transactions as read-only.

## Who Can Do This

- **Financials - View**: can view and download PDFs for published reports
- **Financials - Treasurer** and **Financials - Manage**: can also publish reports

## Viewing Reports

The reports table shows all months (for treasurer/manage) or only published months (for view-only staff). Each row shows:

- Month name
- Total income, total expenses, net change
- Published or Unpublished status
- Action buttons

Click **View** on any published month to open a detail modal showing the full breakdown: income, expenses, account balances as of end of month, and budget variance by category.

## Downloading a PDF

Click **PDF** on any published month (or **Download PDF** inside the view modal) to download a formatted period report as a PDF. The file includes the full income/expense summary, account balances, budget variances, and transactions by category.

## Publishing a Report

Publishing a month makes it visible to all staff and on the public finances page. It also permanently locks all transactions in that month.

1. Find the unpublished month in the reports list.
2. Click **Publish** on that row.
3. A summary modal appears showing the income, expenses, net change, account balances, and budget variances for that month. Review this carefully.
4. Click **Confirm & Publish** to finalize.

A toast confirms the report was published. The month's status changes to **Published** and its transactions are now locked.

## What Happens After Publishing

- All transactions dated in that month become read-only -- edits and deletes are blocked
- The month appears on the public `/finances` page for community members to view
- Staff with Financials - View access can see the published month in the reports list
- The "Finance Report Overdue" warning on meeting pages disappears (for the next 14 days)

## Important Notes

- Publishing is permanent -- you can't unpublish a month once it's been published.
- Make sure all transactions are correct before publishing. Verify the pre-publish summary closely.
- A month with no transactions can't be published.
