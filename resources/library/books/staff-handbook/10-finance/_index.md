How to use the Lighthouse Finance System. This covers recording income and expenses, bank reconciliation, fiscal period management, budgeting, financial reports, and administrative tasks like managing the chart of accounts and restricted funds.

Access to the Finance System is role-based — see [[books/staff-handbook/finance/overview/finance-roles|Finance Roles and Access]] for a breakdown of who can do what.
