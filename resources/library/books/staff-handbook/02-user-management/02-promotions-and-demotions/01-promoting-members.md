## Overview

Beyond the initial Stowaway review, you'll also promote Travelers to Resident and Residents to Citizen as they grow in the community. Promotions are always one level at a time -- you can't skip levels.

## Who Can Do This

- Admins
- Officers (any department)
- Crew Members in the **Quartermaster** department

## Promoting Travelers to Resident

The **Traveler Users** widget on your Dashboard shows all Travelers who aren't in the Brig, sorted by how long ago they were promoted (oldest first). This helps you prioritize members who've been waiting the longest.

1. Open the **Traveler Users** widget on your Dashboard
2. Click a user's name to see their details, including when they were promoted to Traveler
3. Click **Promote to Resident**
4. A success message confirms the promotion

## Promoting from a Profile Page

You can promote any user from their **profile page**:

1. Navigate to the user's profile
2. Click the **three-dot menu** on their profile card
3. Select **Promote to {next level}** (e.g., "Promote to Resident" or "Promote to Citizen")
4. Confirm in the modal that appears

The promote option only appears when:
- You have permission to manage users
- The user is at least Stowaway level
- The user is not in the Brig

## What Happens

Every promotion automatically:
- Updates the user's membership level
- Syncs their **Minecraft rank** on the server
- Syncs their **Discord roles**
- Records the action in the activity log
- Sends a notification to the user (Traveler and Resident promotions)

Citizen promotions do not send a notification to the user.
