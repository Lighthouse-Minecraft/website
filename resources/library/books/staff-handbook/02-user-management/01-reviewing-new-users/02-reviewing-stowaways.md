## Overview

When a new user registers and accepts the community rules, they become a **Stowaway**. They're waiting for a staff member to review their account and promote them to **Traveler**, which grants Minecraft server access and full community features.

## Who Can Do This

- Admins
- Officers (any department)
- Crew Members in the **Quartermaster** department

## How to Review Stowaways

1. Go to your **Dashboard** -- you'll see the **Stowaway Users** widget if you have permission
2. The widget shows a paginated list of all Stowaway users who are not in the Brig
3. Click on a user's name to open their **detail modal**
4. In the detail modal, you can see:
   - When they joined
   - Whether they've linked any Minecraft or Discord accounts
5. If everything looks good, click **Promote to Traveler**
6. You'll see a success message confirming the promotion

## What Happens After Promotion

When you promote a Stowaway to Traveler:

- Their membership level updates immediately
- If they've already linked a Minecraft account, they're automatically whitelisted on the server
- Their Discord roles are updated to reflect the Traveler level
- They receive a notification about their promotion
- The action is logged in the activity log

## When to Hold Off

Sometimes you might not want to promote a user right away. Common reasons:

- Their username seems inappropriate or suspicious
- They just registered moments ago and you want to give it some time
- Something about their account raises concerns

If a user seems problematic, you can **Put in Brig** directly from the Stowaway widget instead of promoting them. See the [[books/staff-handbook/user-management/the-brig/putting-users-in-the-brig|Putting Users in the Brig]] section for details.

## Promoting from the Profile Page

You can also promote users from their **profile page**. Visit their profile, click the three-dot menu, and select **Promote to Traveler**. A confirmation modal will appear before the action is taken. This method works for any promotion level, not just Stowaway to Traveler.
