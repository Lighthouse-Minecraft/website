## Overview

As a Crew Member or above, you can set up a public staff profile that appears on the **Staff Page** and on your user profile. This includes your first name, last initial, a short bio, a photo, and optionally your phone number.

## Who Can Do This

Crew Members, Officers, and Board Members can edit their staff bio. Junior Crew Members cannot -- their listing on the Staff Page shows their position title but not personal details.

## How to Set Up Your Profile

1. Go to **Settings** in the top navigation
2. Click **Staff Bio** in the settings sidebar
3. Fill in the fields you'd like to share:
   - **First Name** -- your real first name (up to 50 characters)
   - **Last Initial** -- a single letter
   - **Bio** -- a short bio about yourself (up to 2,000 characters)
   - **Phone Number** -- optional, only visible to Officers and Board Members
   - **Photo** -- upload a photo of yourself (max 2 MB, image files only)
4. Click **Save Staff Bio**

You can update these fields anytime. To remove your photo, click the **Remove Photo** button that appears below your current photo.

## Where Your Profile Appears

Your staff bio information shows up in two places:

- **The Staff Page** (`/staff`) -- your photo, name, title, rank, and bio are displayed when someone clicks your card in your department's section
- **Your user profile** -- visitors to your profile page will see your staff photo, name, and bio alongside your regular profile information

## Phone Number Privacy

Your phone number is only visible to **Officers** and **Board Members**. Regular members and the public cannot see it. A note on the settings page reminds you of this.

## Important Notes

- If you're later unassigned from your staff position, your bio data (name, photo, bio, phone) is preserved in the system but won't be displayed on the Staff Page since you won't have an active position
- Your staff photo is separate from your regular profile avatar -- it's meant for a more professional or personal photo
- Junior Crew Members have a position listing on the Staff Page, but their real name, bio, and staff photo are hidden from public view until they reach Crew Member rank
