## Welcome Aboard

Congratulations on joining the Lighthouse staff team! You're now part of a volunteer team that keeps this community running smoothly -- from reviewing new members and answering support tickets, to managing the Minecraft server and keeping Discord organized.

This handbook is your go-to reference for how things work behind the scenes. You don't need to memorize it all right now -- come back to it whenever you need a refresher.

## What's Expected of You

Being on staff is a volunteer commitment, not a job. That said, your team depends on you to be reliable. Here's what that looks like:

- **Stay active** -- check in regularly and keep up with your department's tasks
- **Be approachable** -- members see you as a representative of the community
- **Ask questions** -- if you're unsure how to handle something, ask a fellow staff member or your department Officer before acting
- **Use the tools** -- the website has systems for tickets, tasks, meetings, and more. Use them instead of working from memory

## The Staff Ready Room

The **Staff Ready Room** is your staff hub on the website. You can find it in the **Management** section of the side bar once you're logged in as a staff member. It's where you'll spend most of your time.

Inside the Ready Room, you'll find:

- **My Board** -- your personal dashboard showing upcoming meetings and your assigned tasks
- **Department tabs** -- each department has its own tab with department-specific tasks and information. You can see your own department's tab plus any department you have Officer access to

The Ready Room is only visible to staff members (Junior Crew and above).

## Support Tickets

One of your main ways of interacting with community members who need help is through **support tickets**. You can access the ticket queue from the Ready Room or the navigation bar. Tickets are how members ask for help, report issues, or request actions like promotions.

You'll learn the details of handling tickets in the [[books/staff-handbook/support-and-moderation|Support & Moderation]] section of this handbook.

## The Staff Page

There's a public **Staff Page** at [Staff Page]({{url:/staff}}) that shows all staff members organized by department. Members and visitors can see who's on the team, your title, and your bio (if you've set one up).

You'll want to [[books/staff-handbook/getting-started/orientation/your-staff-profile|set up your staff profile]] once you've been promoted to Crew Member rank.
