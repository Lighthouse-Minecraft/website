## Overview

Draft reports are internal until an Officer publishes them. Published reports become visible to the member and their parents, and contribute to the member's risk score.

## Publishing Reports

### Who Can Publish

- Officers (any department)
- Admins

### How to Publish

1. Open the report from the member's profile or the Dashboard widget
2. Click the **Publish** button
3. Confirm in the dialog

You can also publish from the **Discipline Reports log** in the Admin Control Panel, which shows all reports across the community in a filterable, sortable table.

### Staff-Subject Safeguard

If the report is about a **staff member**, the person who created the report **cannot** publish it themselves. Another Officer must review and publish it. This prevents a single staff member from documenting and publishing an action against a colleague without oversight.

## What Happens on Publish

When a report is published:

- The member is **notified** about the report
- If the member is a minor with a linked parent, the **parent is also notified**
- The report becomes visible on the member's profile to them (and their parents)
- The member's **risk score** is recalculated
- The publication date and publishing Officer are recorded

## Risk Scores

The risk score aggregates severity points from published reports across three time windows:

| Window | What It Counts |
|---|---|
| **7-day** | Reports published in the last 7 days |
| **30-day** | Reports published in the last 30 days |
| **90-day** | Reports published in the last 90 days |

The **total** is the sum of all three windows. The score uses color-coded thresholds:

| Total Score | Color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | Grey | No recent incidents |
| 1-10 | Green | Low concern |
| 11-25 | Yellow | Moderate concern |
| 26-50 | Orange | High concern |
| 51+ | Red | Critical |

Risk scores appear on user profiles and in the **Top Risk Users** section of the Dashboard discipline widget, helping staff identify members who may need attention.

## The Dashboard Widget

The discipline reports widget on your Dashboard shows:

- **Recent reports** from the last 7 days
- A count of **pending drafts** waiting for publication
- The **top 5 highest-risk users** with their scores and time-window breakdowns
- A link to the full reports log in the Admin Control Panel

## Important Notes

- Commendation reports (0 points) don't increase risk scores -- they're purely positive documentation
- Risk scores are cached for 24 hours per user and refresh when a new report is published
- Officers can edit any draft report, not just their own, before publication
- Reports can never be deleted -- they're a permanent record
