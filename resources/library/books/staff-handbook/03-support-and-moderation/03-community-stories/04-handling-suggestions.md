## Overview

Citizens can suggest new community questions through a form on the stories page. Suggestions appear in your queue for review.

## Who Can Do This

Chaplain department staff (Jr Crew and above), Command Officers, and Admins.

## Where to Find Suggestions

1. Go to the [Community Stories]({{url:/community-stories}}) page
2. Click the **Manage** tab
3. Click the **Suggestions** sub-tab

A badge on the sub-tab shows the count of pending suggestions.

## Reviewing a Suggestion

Each suggestion shows the question text and who submitted it. You have two options:

- **Approve** -- accepts the suggestion and automatically creates a new **Draft** question with the suggested text. You can then edit the draft, adjust the wording, add a description, and schedule it when you're ready.
- **Reject** -- declines the suggestion. No question is created.

## What Happens When You Approve

- The suggestion is marked as approved with your name and the review time
- A new community question is created in **Draft** status with the suggested text
- The question is linked to the original suggestion and the member who suggested it
- You'll find the new draft in the **Questions** sub-tab, ready for you to edit and schedule

## Important Notes

- Approving a suggestion doesn't immediately publish anything -- it just creates a draft for you to work with
- The member who suggested the question is **not** automatically notified of the outcome
- You can edit the draft question text after approval. The suggestion is just a starting point.
- If the suggestion is close but needs tweaking, approve it and then edit the draft rather than rejecting it
