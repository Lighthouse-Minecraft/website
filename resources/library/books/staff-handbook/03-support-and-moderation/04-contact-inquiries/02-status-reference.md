## Status Overview

Contact inquiries move through four statuses. Pick the one that accurately reflects where things stand -- it helps the whole team understand the queue at a glance.

## Open

The inquiry needs attention. Use **Open** when:

- The inquiry just came in and hasn't been responded to yet.
- A guest has replied and the conversation is still active.
- You've re-opened a closed thread because new information came in.

Guests can reply freely when an inquiry is Open.

## Pending

You've responded but you're waiting on something before you can close it out. Use **Pending** when:

- You've asked the guest a follow-up question and are waiting for their answer.
- You need to consult with another staff member or department before resolving.
- You've passed the inquiry to someone else and are waiting for an update.

Guests can still reply when an inquiry is Pending.

## Resolved

The matter is settled, but you're keeping the thread available for a short time in case the guest has follow-up questions. Use **Resolved** when:

- You've given a complete answer and believe no reply is needed.
- You want to signal "done" to the team but aren't ready to archive it yet.

Guests cannot submit new replies to a Resolved thread. If they respond by email, they'll need to submit a new inquiry from the Contact Us form.

## Closed

The inquiry is fully archived. Use **Closed** when:

- The issue has been resolved and no further activity is expected.
- The thread is old and no longer needs to appear in the active queue.

Neither staff nor guests can reply to a Closed thread. Staff can always re-open it by switching the status back to **Open** or **Pending**.
