The Lighthouse Policy Manual contains the official policies that govern our community. These policies apply to all members and are publicly accessible so that anyone — prospective members, parents, or current community members — can review them without needing to log in.

This manual is organized into four parts covering community standards, safety and privacy, moderation practices, and staff operations.
