# First Steps

Welcome to Lighthouse! Here's what to do first:

1. **Create your account** on the website
2. **Set up your profile** with basic information
3. **Link your Minecraft account** to access the server
4. **Join our Discord** to connect with the community

Once you've completed these steps, you're ready to start your journey!
