# First Steps

Welcome to Lighthouse! Here's what to do first:

1. **[Create your account]({{url:/library/books/user-handbook/website/your-account/creating-your-account}})** on the website
2. **Read & Agree** to the rules on the [Dashboard]({{url:/dashboard}})
3. **[Link your Minecraft account]({{url:/library/books/user-handbook/minecraft/accounts/linking-your-account}})** to access the server
4. **[Link your Discord account]({{url:/library/books/user-handbook/discord/accounts/linking-your-account}})** to connect with the community

Once you've completed these steps, you're ready to start your journey!
